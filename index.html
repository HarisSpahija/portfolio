Who am I?

Motivated, Curious and Organised. That is how my colleagues would describe me. I work hard for every project to make sure I will not only satisfy my customers, but also myself. The goal of all my projects is to learn and develop new skill. Iterating on oneselve is key to becomming a succesful developer. With a background in gamedevelopment and a big interest in Mobile applications I try to make applications that will not only interest others, but also myself.

You see, as a developer you will offer the best UX if you use the application yourself aswell. If I make a game I will know it will be a success if the team plays the game in their spare time. If something bothers me during the day I will ask myself how I can potentionally make something that will solve my problem or frustrations.
